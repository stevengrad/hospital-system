🎭 Face Recognition Model Training

Train a model to identify who the person is (not just detect faces)

👁️ Face Detection

Question: "Where are the faces?"

Output: Bounding boxes

Example: YOLO, RetinaFace

Use: Find faces in images

🎭 Face Recognition

Question: "Who is this person?"

Output: Identity/Name

Example: FaceNet, ArcFace

Use: Identify specific people

📋 Training Overview

Goal: Train a model to create unique embeddings for each person's face

Method: Metric learning with triplet/ArcFace loss

Input: Multiple images per person (5-20 photos each)

Output: Model that generates 512-dim embeddings for comparison

Time: 4-12 hours depending on dataset size

📊 Dataset Requirements

  • 5-20 images per person
  • Minimum 10 people
  • 100-1000 total identities ideal
  • Different angles & lighting
  • Clear, frontal faces

🏗️ Model Architecture

  • Backbone: ResNet, MobileNet
  • Embedding: 128/256/512-dim
  • Loss: Triplet, ArcFace, CosFace
  • Output: Normalized embeddings
  • Comparison: Cosine similarity

🎯 Training Process

  • Metric learning approach
  • Triplet/pair sampling
  • Hard negative mining
  • Batch size: 32-128
  • Epochs: 50-200

✅ Evaluation Metrics

  • Verification accuracy
  • LFW benchmark: >99%
  • TAR@FAR curves
  • Embedding separation
  • Inference speed
